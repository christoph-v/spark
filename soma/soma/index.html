What is the Connection between S&P-ARK and SOMA

S&P-ARK aims to become a kind of community that will learn more about the technologies that will be relevant for X3D4 Multiuser Scenes.

Such a community could be efficiently supported by a kind of "official" association, which we would call "Society for Lifelong Collaborating" (please refer to https://lc-soc-lc.or.at for details.

Such an association would need tools for the internal communication, where we come to invent SOMA:

Simple OwnCloud based Management of Associations
